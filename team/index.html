About Sidharth

Sidharth Kriplani is a student pursuing BTech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. He began his toastmasters journey on 5th April at Central Delhi Toastmasters Club. He is deeply committed to Toastmasters and grateful to it for the platform it provides, focusing on communication and leadership skills exclusively through the prestigious platform of public speaking. Having come this far from 0 to 1, he knows the value of the Toastmasters journey and always tries to make everyone around him share his belief. With infectious energy and confidence, he likes interacting with people and motvating them to challenge themselves at every step of their lives. Sidharth loves reading, working out, learning new things and most of all, public speaking. His life's simple mantra is to give all that you have got to whatever it is that you want.

About Kreeti

Kreeti is a supply chain professional working with IKEA, the world's largest furniture retailer. She has pursued MBA from IIFT and engineering from MSIT. She loves travelling as it introduces her to new people, cultures, food and ways of life. She pursues adventures sports such as rafting, skydiving and trekking. She also enjoys long distance running. Rather, she club her love for travelling, long distance running and toastmasters, by running half marathons in newer cities and visiting their local toastmasters clubs. She believes joining Toastmasters was one of the best decisions in her life. She believes Toastmasters has given her an excellent platform to sharpen her communication and leadership skills. The invaluable lessons learnt in toastmasters are used directly in our professional and personal life, giving us more confidence and success!
